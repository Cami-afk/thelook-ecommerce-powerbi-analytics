# Measures used in the report (Power BI)

Below is a short list of the key measures implemented in the dedicated **Measures Table** in Power BI.

## Sales & Orders
- Revenue
- Revenue (Completed Orders)
- Average Basket (Revenue per Order)
- Order Count
- Average Sale Price

## Customers
- Customers (Distinct users)
- Average Amount of Orders (Orders per customer)
- Multiple Customers / Single Customer split (as used in the report)

## Geography
- CountryPopulation (reference table)
- Market Penetration (Customers vs. population)
